Our Story

Born from a love of warmth & craft

تلقيمه is more than a bakery. It is a gathering place. We believe that the best moments happen over a perfectly brewed cup of tea and something freshly baked. Every item on our menu is made with intention, from our signature Karak to our artisan bratas.

We source the finest ingredients and bring together recipes that honor tradition while celebrating creativity.
